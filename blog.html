About single Page

A Single Page website does not contain additional pages like About, Team or Services. All the content remain within the same webpage. The beauty of a single Page website is it tries to promote one thing in a direct manner. This allows the visitor to make a quicker view vs sending them to a bloated website with many options and pages.

When? Who?

The One Page Love website was launched in March 2008 by Rob Hope in Cape Town, South Africa who continues to add One Page websites.
